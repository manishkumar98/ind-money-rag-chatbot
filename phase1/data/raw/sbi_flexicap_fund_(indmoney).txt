About SBI Flexicap Fund

SBI Flexicap Fund is an equity fund. This fund was started on 1 January, 2013. The fund is managed by Anup Upadhyay. The fund could potentially beat inflation in the long-run.

Key Parameters
SBI Flexicap Fund has ₹20626 Cr worth of assets under management (AUM) as on Apr 2026 and is more than category average.
The fund has an expense ratio 0.9.

Returns

SBI Flexicap Fund has given a CAGR return of 14.91% since inception. Over the last 1, 3 and 5 years the fund has given a CAGR return of 3.60%, 11.90% and 11.77% respectively.

Holdings

SBI Flexicap Fund has allocated its funds majorly in Cash Equivalent, Financial Services, Basic Materials, Consumer Cyclical, Industrial, Energy, Tech, Health, Utilities, Government, Communication, Consumer Defensive. Its top holdings are Axis Bank Ltd, HDFC Bank Ltd, State Bank of India, ICICI Bank Ltd, Larsen & Toubro Ltd

Taxation

As it is a flexi cap mutual fund the taxation is as follows:
For short term (less than a year) capital gains will be taxed at 20%
For long term (more than 1 year) capital gains will be taxed at 12.5% without indexation benefit
Dividends will always be taxed at slab rate. Long term gains upto Rs 1 lakh are exempt capital gains tax.


Investment objective of SBI Flexicap Fund

To provide investors with opportunities for longterm growth in capital along with the liquidity of an open-ended scheme through an active management of investments in a diversified basket of equity stocks spanning the entire market capitalization spectrum and in debt and money market instruments.

Minimum Investment and lockin period

Minimum investment for lump sum payment is INR 1000.00 and for SIP is INR 500.00. SBI Flexicap Fund has no lock in period.
